Privacy Policy for Password Generator & Strength Checker Chrome Extension

Last Updated: 06/10/2025

Thank you for using the Password Generator & Strength Checker Chrome extension ("the Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Extension.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The Extension is designed with your privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any passwords you generate or check.
  • We do not track your browsing activity or history.
  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

2. How the Extension Works

The Extension operates entirely within your browser:

  • All password generation and strength checking happens locally on your device.
  • No password data is sent to any servers or third parties.
  • The only external permission required is "clipboardWrite" to enable the copy-to-clipboard functionality.

3. Data Storage

The Extension does not store any passwords or user data:

  • Generated passwords exist only temporarily in your browser's memory while the extension is open.
  • No data is saved to your device's storage or to any cloud services.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the Chrome Web Store listing for the Extension. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
